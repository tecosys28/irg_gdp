Activate Your Wallet

You cannot transact until your wallet is activated. Activation is a one-time process covering four things:

  1. Write down your 15-word recovery phrase — the only way to recover your wallet if you lose your device.
  2. Set a wallet password (different from your login password).
  3. Register at least two nominees.
  4. Declare the personal law that governs succession of your assets.

Step 1: Your 15-word recovery phrase

IMPORTANT. These 15 words are the ONLY way to recover your wallet if you lose your phone or forget your password.
  • Write them on paper. Do not screenshot them.
  • Store the paper somewhere safe and private.
  • Never type them into any website or share them with anyone — not even IRG staff.

Step 4: Wallet password

Must be at least 8 characters with a letter and a digit — OR a 4-word passphrase of 20+ characters. Must be different from your login password.

Step 5: Register nominees (minimum 2, shares must total 100%)

Required for individual wallets. Your nominees can help recover access if you lose your device and forget your password.

Optional for legal-person wallets. If provided, they serve as the humans who can initiate recovery on behalf of the entity.

Step 6: Accept recovery terms

By activating, you confirm you understand:
  • IRG stores only a hash of your password and recovery phrase. We cannot reset them.
  • If you lose access: self-recovery via seed phrase, or nominee-assisted recovery, or trustee-assisted recovery via the IRG Ombudsman.
  • For legal-person wallets, ownership transfer (acquisition, partnership change, trustee succession) runs through a dedicated Ombudsman-reviewed process.
  • In case of your death, your nominees and legal heirs can recover your assets through the documented process.
