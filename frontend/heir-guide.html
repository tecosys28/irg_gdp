How to recover an IRG wallet for a family member

If someone you love held assets in an IRG wallet and has passed away, become incapacitated, or lost access in a way that cannot be resolved, this page explains how to claim those assets on their behalf.

You do not need to be logged in to read this page. IRG will never ask you for the deceased person's seed phrase or password. If anyone asks, that is a scam.
